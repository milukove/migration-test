the science behind the sit

Anthros was born from a desire to revolutionize sitting, inspired by experts in wheelchair seating who wanted to create a better solution for everyone. So we designed Anthros — the first chair built on "Anthronomics," a unique blend of biomechanics, ergonomics, and anthropometrics.

After hearing the same comment at industry trade shows—“I wish my office chair felt like this!”—we realized the world deserved more than just another “ergonomic” chair.

Designed to adapt to the human body, Anthros brings comfort and support like never before.
